We Are Power church of Christ

Our Purpose.

To glorify God and to make disciples of Jesus Christ. This is what the Bible teaches us in the Great Commission: "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age" (Matthew 28:19-20).

Our Mission.

To glorify God and to make disciples of Jesus Christ. worshiping God together, learning about His Word, growing in our faith, and sharing the good news with others. To serve the community and work to promote justice and peace. also to diverse community of people from all walks of life, and united by our common faith in Jesus Christ.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV
